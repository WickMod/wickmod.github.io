Who Will Put A Screeching Hault To The Intergalactic Planetary Conquest Perpetrated By The Freaky Evil BIOMORGS

(Or Just "Biomorgs" for short)

is a singleplayer first person shooter I created in Unreal Engine 5 using blueprints. Defeat alien "Biomorgs" who use RTS base-building mechanics.

The prototype of this game was developed over the span of 6 weeks for my Realtime Programming class. Continued development is underway to add exciting new features and polish the user experience.

Game Design

The goal of Biomorgs is to provide a cathartic and CRUNCHY gaming experience.

Two main play styles are supported. The player can strategically cut off the enemies resources and cripple their supply chain before dismantling the enemy base OR the the player can storm in with gun in hand, sledge hammer on back, and zero shits given exploding everything in sight and overpowering the enemy with pure skill alone.

The CRUNCHY gameplay is supported by JUICEY destruction physics and effects. Upon death, and enemy becomes one rigid physics object to be kicked over, dragged, or launched into the air. When an enemy structure is destroyed, it explodes into all it's component pieces. It also emmits a powerful explosion effect using boxy particles that harken back to the game's low poly aesthetic.

Assets

All of the 3D assets except for temporary Unreal Engine Manequin player model were created by me in Blender.
